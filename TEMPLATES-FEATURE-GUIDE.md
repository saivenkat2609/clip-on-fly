# Templates Feature Guide

## Overview

The Templates section is a powerful feature in Reframe AI that provides users with professionally designed video templates to quickly enhance their AI-generated clips. Instead of manually styling each clip, users can apply pre-designed templates with one click, dramatically reducing editing time while maintaining professional quality.

---

## What Are Templates?

Templates are pre-designed video styles that include:
- **Typography & Text Styling**: Font choices, sizes, colors, and animations for captions
- **Color Schemes**: Coordinated color palettes that match specific moods or brand identities
- **Layout Designs**: Positioning of captions, overlays, and visual elements
- **Animations & Transitions**: Professional motion graphics and text animations
- **Visual Effects**: Background patterns, overlays, and decorative elements
- **Branding Elements**: Logo placement areas and brand-consistent styling

---

## How Templates Work in Your Workflow

### **Step 1: Generate Clips**
Upload your long-form video → AI generates short clips with optimal engagement scores

### **Step 2: Browse Templates**
Navigate to the Templates section → Browse by category or search → Preview templates

### **Step 3: Apply Template**
Select a clip → Choose a template → Click "Use Template" → Instant styling applied

### **Step 4: Customize (Optional)**
Fine-tune colors, fonts, or positioning to match your exact needs

### **Step 5: Export & Share**
Download your professionally styled clip ready for posting

- Match template category to content type and audience expectations

## Getting Started

1. **Navigate to Templates** from the main menu
2. **Browse categories** or use search to find specific styles
3. **Preview templates** by hovering over thumbnails
4. **Note Popular & Trending badges** for proven designs
5. **Click "Use Template"** on any clip from your dashboard
6. **Customize if needed** in the editor
7. **Export and share** your professionally styled content
